At TransitScreen, we believe in the power of information to help people make smarter decisions about how they get around cities.

What we do

TransitScreen displays all your mobility options, at a glance, in real time.

Compare train times to bus arrivals on your way to work in the morning. Know when your employee shuttle is coming so you aren’t caught waiting in the cold. Grab another refill at the coffee shop while you wait for your streetcar. Catch an Uber instead of using bikeshare if it’s raining.

Our service is active in every major city in the US and Canada, plus select European cities. We support more than 10 languages, including English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, Italian, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Hindi.

How we started

TransitScreen launched in 2013 from a civic technology project at Mobility Lab in Arlington, Virginia, focused on urban mobility solutions.

Our team grew out of the 1776 incubator and is now headquartered in downtown Washington, DC.

We take great pride in how we build our products while maintaining our values: a focus on environmental sustainability, a commitment to open data and standards, and increasing accessibility to all people.

Who we are

We believe in creating smarter, more sustainable cities, and we hire people who live these values every day. None of our employees drives alone to work, and we take pride in being a 100% carbon neutral company.

We fully support open standards, and we believe in contributing to open data and open source efforts whenever we can.
